What is AllergyScanner?

This app allows you to scan barcodes, view ingredient lists, and detect allergens based on your personal allergy profile. Stay safe by making informed decisions about the products you consume.

How to Use AllergyScanner

  • Step 1: Sign up or log in to create your allergy profile.
  • Step 2: Scan a product barcode to extract the ingredient list.
  • Step 3: The app will notify you if any allergens are detected.
